PRIMUS Global Solutions – Company Overview
Founded in 2002, PRIMUS Global Solutions is a global IT services and consulting organization delivering enterprise-grade technology solutions and strategic talent services across North America, Europe, the Middle East, and India.

With a global talent pool of over 10,000 professionals—including PRIMUS Global Solutions and its Centers of Excellence (CoEs)—PRIMUS supports 400+ clients by combining deep domain expertise, emerging technologies, and agile delivery models.
